Why LSTM v2 is Selected as Champion:
  v2 improves on v1 by adding 11 diagnosis-driven features:
    - is_april: flags Easter-season high-error month
    - rolling_14_sales, rolling_7_std_sales: medium-term trend + volatility
    - promo_schoolholiday, weekend_schoolholiday, promo_weekend: interaction signals
    - days_since_last_promo, days_until_next_promo: promo timing context
    - is_month_start, is_month_end: payday and clearance effects
    - sales_vs_lag7: short-term demand momentum (past features only)
  The deeper future encoder (2-layer MLP vs 1-layer in v1) better handles
  the non-linear interactions between these richer future features.
  Early stopping and L2 weight decay prevent the larger feature set
  from causing overfitting.
